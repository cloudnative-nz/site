Bridging The Digital Divide

The New Zealand Government estimates that up to one in five New Zealanders experience some form of digital exclusion due to a lack of access, affordability, skills, trust, or the motivation to get online - Salmon, Kaleah. 2023

Our solution

  • Instant infrastructre (hardware + software) across new zealand equipped with learning environments that give the best possible chance of succesful learning and engagement in the digital world for all new zealanders.
  • A collobaritive interconnected educational platform to learn and create without the need for exspensive equipment, you can use ours for free!
  • Open and inclusive environment for our tamariki to thrive and find their own passions and interests
  • No internet connection is needed, we can set you up a local area network to use within your community

The Implementation

  • We are createing inclusive and culturally ethical collaborative spaces equipped with the needed resources for both kids and teachers to succeed in the tech space.
  • We will partner with schools and community spaces to setup and demo the spaces we have created.
  • Cross regional open source projects that align with the accreddited curriculm to engage the children as a cohort by working on real world problems that benefit their community.

Our Mission

Te Whainga

Technology continues to influence the world of work.

This means immense opportunity for future careers and learning, but also an increased need to ensure our people are well-equipped to get involved.

We believe to effectively support growth opportunities in the tech sector, it needs a community-centric approach that bridges transitional gaps between individuals, education, industry and government, while upholding the hauora (wellbeing) of potential Contributors.

' Kupenga ' is a pilot Te Ao Māori mentoring initiative we're developing to make it easier for our people to contribute to Open Source and position Aotearoa in the future of Cloud.

What is Open Source?

Open Source is computer code all users can inspect, modify and enhance. Because it’s ‘open’, you can own it, create, and innovate.

At its core, open source embraces and celebrates principles of open exchange, collaboration, transparency and community-oriented development.

The result is more than just tech, but a unique way of interacting with one another to innovate solutions and shape the world around us for the better.
